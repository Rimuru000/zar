About us

Welcome to Caramel Cascade Delight!!
Located in the heart of Mangilik El C1, Caramel Cascade Delight is the brainchild of three passionate individuals: M. Zhuldyz, T. Alik, and I. Rustem. With a shared love for exceptional coffee and a dedication to creating an extraordinary experience, we embarked on a journey to redefine the coffee culture in our community. At Caramel Cascade Delight, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage—it's an art form. Our team of skilled baristas meticulously crafts each cup, combining the finest beans, precise techniques, and a touch of creativity to deliver a moment of pure bliss with every sip.
