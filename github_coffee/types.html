Types of coffee


Espresso

A concentrated shot of coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely ground.



Americano

A shot of espresso diluted with hot water, creating a similar strength to drip coffee.



Latte

Espresso mixed with steamed milk and topped with a small amount of milk foam.



Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a balanced and creamy drink.



Macchiato

Indulge in the perfect balance of strong espresso shots and creamy steamed milk, creating experience.


Flat White

Indulge in the perfect harmony of espresso and microfoam milk, resulting in a velvety smooth texture.


Frappuccino

A blended coffee drink made with ice, milk, and flavored syrups. It often includes whipped cream on top.


Turkish Coffee

A traditional method of brewing coffee where finely ground coffee is boiled in a special pot called a cezve.
