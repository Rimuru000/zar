Espresso

A concentrated shot of coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans.

Americano

A shot of espresso diluted with hot water, creating a similar strength to drip coffee.

Latte

Espresso mixed with steamed milk and topped with a small amount of milk foam.

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a balanced and creamy drink.

Macchiato

Espresso "stained" with a small amount of milk or milk foam.

Flat White

Espresso combined with microfoam milk, creating a velvety and smooth texture.

Frappuccino

A blended coffee drink made with ice, milk, and flavored syrups. It often includes whipped cream on top.

Turkish Coffee

A traditional method of brewing coffee where finely ground coffee is boiled in a special pot called a cezve.

Lungo

It refers to a type of coffee that is made by extracting more water through the coffee grounds than in a regular espresso shot.

Doppio

To prepare a doppio, two separate shots of espresso are pulled using a double-portafilter and a double dose of finely ground coffee.

Drip Coffee

Regular brewed coffee made by pouring hot water over coffee grounds in a filter.

Affogato

A scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato "drowned" with a shot of hot espresso.
